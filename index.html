22 Park Street · Easthampton, MA 01027 · (203) 444-5097 · berghoc@clarkson.edu

Chris Berghoff (born March 1995, CT USA) is a Game Designer and Digital Artist primarily based in creative development, algorithms, and data. With backgrounds in Computer Science, Visual Arts, Social Media, and even Technical Theatre, he tackles a multitude of various projects and ambitions. He is currently pursuing a degree in Digital Arts and Sciences at Clarkson University.

Artist Statement

Chris is a jack of all trades in creative mediums and design. Primarily geared towards Game Design, he conceives inspiration and ideas from creative brainstorming, almost as if he were daydreaming. These ideas are then quantified or documented, allowing them to be flushed out primarily in programs like Google Sheets and Docs. Beyond game design, Chris dabbles in multiple artistic and design-based platforms, such as Maya, Photoshop, Unity, Premier, and Java coding. Whether it be games, art, or media, he implements algorithms and data analysis/interaction in almost everything he does. His philosophy is that no matter how beautiful, complex, or abstract an idea or visual is, there are procedures and equations influencing the content whether they be designed or unintentional. Melding wild creativity and mathematical practice into a cohesive design is a challenge Chris excels at, striving to achieve a unique yet methodically calculated product.
